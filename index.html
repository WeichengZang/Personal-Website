Intro

Welcome! My name is Weicheng Zang (臧伟呈). I am currently a CfA fellowat Center for Astrophysics | Harvard & Smithsonian. Previously I obtained my PhD in Astronomy from Department of Astronomy, Tsinghua University, under the supervision of Prof. Shude Mao. I was born in Beijing (北京), China and my hometown is Wenzhou (温州), China. My main research interest is using the Gravitational microlensing technique to study extrasolar planets, binary stars and stellar remnants (e.g., black hole).
